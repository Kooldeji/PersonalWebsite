Nostalgia!


An Irresistible Sensation

Yes! Nostalgia deprives us of the horrors of the old,
It smoothens the edges of the good old days,
leaving us with sweet memories we cannot control.
But aren’t those the memories that we crave.

Years will come,
the years of our children,
the years our children would communicate without the internet,
the years we just would not comprehend.
Then we’d remember the years of Coca-Cola,
the years of YouTube,
the years of Facebook and Instagram,
and yearn for these years.

Then You’d understand the meaning of “NOSTALGIA”!!

Music!


Music has no language

Music is the basic phenomenon in which everything functions, Okay Let’s argue.
What is Music? Music is a pleasant sequence of sounds, organized in time.

What is sound? Sound is a sensation, caused by vibration of air or some other medium. Every object in nature has what the scientists call a “Natural Vibration”, so in other words, every object vibrates at a natural frequency

Music is Platform Independent, it understands no language. Music communicates at the atomic level of nature.
